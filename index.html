What are Altarius?

Altarius is an friendly online community of the server burning legion. Altarius was founded the first of august 2017 and primaly focus on PvE based content such as Mythic Dungeons & Raiding. We are an english guild whom always strive for progression. However we do not require all our fellow members to be focusing PvE as the guild is, with that said we are always looking for assets to the guild in the way of cheerfull spirits to join our community. We always try to push our limits progression wise may that be in either reaching a new boss upper the difficulty.

But what different ourselfes from the other hundreds of PvE Based raiding guilds?
We are always looking to assist our fellow members by scheduling weekly alt/boost raid runs together with mythic dungeon boost. We're also making sure that Altarius is living up to our expectations through all of our members may that be New Member Bob or high officer PhantomLord. To ensure that Altarius lives up to our expectations we have an open discord server to everyone to come together regardless rank, prefered content or interests as a community.
